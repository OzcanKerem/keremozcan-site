I am an Electrical and Electronics Engineer currently pursuing my M.Sc. at Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University. My work spans FPGA-based digital design, quantum circuits, advanced optoelectronic applications, CMOS, digital integrated circuits and optical fiber communication systems.

Alongside academic research, I actively work with embedded systems, circuit design, and applied engineering problems. I also have hands-on experience with smart home technologies, solar energy systems, and automatic door and access control systems, combining field expertise with solid engineering foundations. In parallel, I maintain an active interest in amateur radio technologies and RF systems.

Quantum Projects

Quantum circuit concepts, modeling, and hardware-aware thinking (growing portfolio).

Quantum Circuits — Study Notes & Models
I designed a two-qubit transmon system using Qiskit Metal, focusing on layout-level modeling and circuit-based interpretation of superconducting qubits. The design explores qubit geometry, capacitive coupling, and junction placement to achieve controlled interaction while maintaining symmetry and fabrication awareness. Through electromagnetic-informed layout decisions, the system provides a compact and analyzable platform for studying qubit–qubit coupling, frequency allocation, and readout-oriented design considerations.
